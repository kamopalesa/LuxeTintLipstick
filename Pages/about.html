ABOUT LUXETINT LIPSTICK:

Our story:

LuxeTint Lipstick was founded in 2025 in Polokwane with the mission to provide high-quality, sustainable cosmetics. We believe beauty should be both empowering and environmentally conscious.

Our values:

We are committed to producing vegan, cruelty-free lipsticks with eco-friendly packaging. Our products are designed to help people express themselves with confidence and style.

Our mission:

To inspire confidence and self-expression by offering high-quality lipsticks that are both stylish and environmentally friendly.

Our vision:

To become South Africans leading ethical lipstick brand , known for innovation, sustainability and empowering beauty lovers everywhere.

Meet the Founder:

Kamogelo Palesa Masiapata founded LuxeTint with passion for creating sustainable beauty products that celebrate individuality. Her vision is to combine style with ethics, proving that beauty can be both glamorous and responsible.
